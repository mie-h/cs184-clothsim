Part5 :

  • Explain in your own words what is a shader program and how vertex and fragment shaders work together to create lighting and material effects.

A shade program utilises the combination of vertex shaders and fragment shaders. It makes the object with moving motion have texture for more real visual effect. For each fragment, after the rasterization, take in the geometric property and calculate the color of the fragment. Vertex shaders utilize the geometric property of each fragment and the value calculated from fragment shading. They apply the effect by transforming the vertex. Diffuse Shading, Blinn-Phong Shading, Texture Mapping, Displacement and Bump Mapping, and Mirror effect are taken into account in this process.

  • Explain the Blinn-Phong shading model in your own words. Show a screenshot of your Blinn-Phong shader outputting only the ambient component, a screen shot only outputting the diffuse component, a screen shot only outputting the specular component, and one using the entire Blinn-Phong model.

Phong shading is shading calculated from taking the ambient light, the diffuse reflection and the specular reflection and adding them up. Blinn-phong shading is phong shading except it uses the calculating of halfway vectors between the camera and the light sources, instead of the dot products over and over.
